Sinigang na Hipon

Fast, fresh, and savory. Large prawns simmered with vegetables in a sharp, citrusy tamarind broth.

Prep: 10m
Cook: 15m
Servings: 3-4
Other Sinigang styles:
Sinigang na Baboy Sinigang na Bangus

Ingredients

  • 500g Large Shrimp or Prawns
  • 1 pack Sinigang Mix
  • 1 bunch String Beans (Sitaw)
  • 1 small Radish, sliced
  • 2 pieces Tomatoes
  • 1 medium Onion
  • 6 cups Water

Instructions

1

Boil water with onions and tomatoes until they are soft.

2

Add the radish and string beans. Cook for 3-4 minutes.

3

Add the sinigang mix and stir until dissolved.

4

Add the prawns. Cook for 3-5 minutes only until they turn orange (don't overcook!).

5

Add kangkong if desired, cover, and serve immediately.
