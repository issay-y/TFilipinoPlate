Filipino Chicken Adobo

The unofficial national dish of the Philippines. Tender chicken marinated in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic, simmered to perfection.

Prep: 15m
Cook: 40m
Servings: 4
Try other versions:
Pork Adobo Adobong Puti Adobong Sitaw

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Chicken (thighs and drumsticks)
  • 1/2 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/3 cup Cane Vinegar
  • 6 cloves Garlic, crushed
  • 3 pieces Dried Bay Leaves
  • 1 tsp Whole Peppercorns
  • 1 cup Water
  • 2 tbsp Cooking Oil
  • 1 tsp Sugar (optional)

Instructions

1

In a large bowl, marinate chicken in soy sauce and garlic for at least 30 minutes.

2

Heat oil in a pan. Pan-fry the marinated chicken for a few minutes until browned. Set aside marinade.

3

Pour in the remaining marinade and water. Add bay leaves and peppercorns. Bring to a boil.

4

Lower heat, cover, and simmer for 25-30 minutes until chicken is tender.

5

Add vinegar. Allow it to boil for 2 minutes without stirring to cook off the raw sharpness.

6

Simmer until sauce thickens or reduces to your liking. Serve hot with steamed rice.
