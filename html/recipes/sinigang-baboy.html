Classic Nilaga / Sour Soup

Sinigang na Baboy

The ultimate comfort food. Tender pork belly simmered in a signature sour tamarind broth with crisp local vegetables.

Prep: 15m
Cook: 1h
Servings: 4-6
Other Sinigang styles:
Sinigang na Hipon Sinigang na Bangus

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Pork Belly (Liempo), cubed
  • 1 pack Sinigang Mix (Tamarind base)
  • 2 pieces Gabi (Taro), quartered
  • 1 bunch Kangkong (Water Spinach)
  • 1 large Radish, sliced
  • 2 medium Tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 large Onion, quartered
  • 3 pieces Long Green Chili (Siling Haba)
  • 8-10 cups Water

Instructions

1

In a large pot, combine water, pork, onions, and tomatoes. Bring to a boil and simmer until pork is tender.

2

Add the gabi (taro) and simmer for another 10-15 minutes until it begins to soften and thicken the soup.

3

Pour in the sinigang mix. Adjust the amount based on your preferred level of sourness.

4

Add the radish and green chilies. Cook for 5 minutes.

5

Add the kangkong leaves, cover the pot, and turn off the heat. Let the residual heat wilt the greens. Serve hot.
