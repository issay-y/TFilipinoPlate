Sinigang na Bangus

A healthy, refreshing seafood stew featuring milkfish. The belly fat of the bangus melts into the sour tamarind broth for a rich finish.

Prep: 15m
Cook: 25m
Servings: 4
Other Sinigang styles:
Sinigang na Baboy Sinigang na Hipon

Ingredients

  • 1 large Bangus (Milkfish), sliced
  • 1 pack Sinigang Mix
  • 1 bunch Mustasa (Mustard Greens)
  • 1 medium Radish, sliced
  • 2 pieces Tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 medium Ginger, sliced
  • 1 large Onion, sliced
  • 8 cups Water

Instructions

1

Boil water with ginger, onions, and tomatoes. The ginger helps remove the fishy scent.

2

Add the radish and simmer for 5 minutes.

3

Carefully add the bangus slices and sinigang mix. Avoid over-stirring to keep the fish intact.

4

Once the fish is cooked, add the mustasa leaves.

5

Turn off the heat and let the leaves cook in the steam for 2 minutes. Serve immediately.
