Our Story

Bringing the heart of the Filipino kitchen to your daily table.

Why we started The Filipino Plate

We know the daily struggle of answering the question, "Anong ulam natin ngayon?" (What's our meal today?). The Filipino Plate was born out of a desire to make meal planning simple, exciting, and deeply rooted in our rich culinary heritage.

Whether you are craving the comforting warmth of a slow-cooked Nilaga on a rainy day, or the quick, savory punch of a Ginisa after a long day at work, we are here to guide you. We categorize dishes not just by ingredients, but by the cooking styles that define Filipino cuisine.

Our Mission

To preserve authentic recipes, inspire modern home cooks, and ensure that no family has to settle for a repetitive meal plan. Discover, plan, and enjoy your daily ulam with us.
